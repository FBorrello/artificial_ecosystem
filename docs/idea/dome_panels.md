# Dome Panels Detailed Specifications

This document provides detailed specifications for the ETFE cushioned panels in the 10-layer dome. Layers 1-7 have 40 gores each (40 panels per layer), layers 8-9 have 20 gores each (20 panels per layer). Panels are double-layer cushions inflated for smooth surface, tapering from base to apex.

The total meridional arc is 23.56 m, divided into 10 equal segments of 2.356 m each. Dimensions are approximate, based on spherical geometry.

## ETFE Panels Specifications
- **Parts**: Two ETFE foil layers per cushion, inflation system.
- **Materials**: ETFE foil (100-200 micron per layer), UV stable, 95% light transmission.
- **Assembly and Manufacturing**: Cut two layers to trapezoid shape, weld perimeter, inflate to 0.1-0.2 bar for cushioning.
- **Specifications**: Inflated cushions press for smooth surface, puncture-resistant, lifespan 25+ years.

## Panel Assembly Process
1. Cut two ETFE sheets to trapezoid dimensions using CNC.
2. Weld perimeter to form cushion, add inflation valve.
3. Inflate to form air gap, attach to frames.
4. Quality check: Inflation test, UV transmission.

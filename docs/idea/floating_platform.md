# Floating Platform Detailed Specifications

This document details the floating platform, a 20m diameter structure with 3 levels floating on the water reservoir.

The platform rotates following the sun, with levels for living, garden, and underwater spaces.

## Design Specifications
- **Shape**: Circular, 20m diameter.
- **Levels**: 3 - underwater, above water living, garden.
- **Rotation**: Water discharge propulsion.
- **Materials**: Buoyant materials, steel frame.

## Functions

- Garden support.
- Solar tracking.

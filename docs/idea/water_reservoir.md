# Main Water Reservoir Detailed Specifications

This document details the main water reservoir, a 20m diameter, 5m deep tank enclosed under the dome, hosting aquatic life and integrated compressed air cylinders.

## Overview
The reservoir stores water for the ecosystem, supports fish/shrimp/bacteria, and houses 6 air cylinders for compressed air storage.

## Design Specifications
- **Shape**: Circular tank.
- **Dimensions**: 20m diameter, 5m depth, volume ~1,570 m³.
- **Materials**: Reinforced concrete walls.
- **Integrated Cylinders**: 6 cylinders, 2.5m dia, 5m deep, at 10+ bar.

## Functions
- Water storage and circulation.
- Aquatic habitat.
- Air storage.

## Cost Estimation
- Construction: ~500,000 €.
- Cylinders: ~100,000 €.
- Total: ~600,000 €.</content>
